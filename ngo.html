Our Activities

  • Drug Bank for kidney failure patients.
  • Medicine Collection Box for distribution to the poor & needy free of charge.
  • Medicines worth Rs. 1 Cr. distributed to other Medicine Banks in Bhavnagar & Surat.
  • Old & wearable clothes distributed to 50 NGOs for the poor.
  • Costly Cancer Medicines provided free to the needy.
  • Medical Equipment like wheelchairs, walkers, and oxygen cylinders available on deposit & hire.
  • Publication of public utility information.
  • Financial support for heart, kidney, and cancer patients through recommendation letters to other trusts.
  • Support for NGOs working with widows, adivasi, unmarried mothers, mentally ill, CP child homes, and more.
  • Support for last stage cancer patients and their rehabilitation.
  • Support to 200 NGOs across various illness categories.

All activities are supervised by a highly qualified individual with 40 years of dedication to the community.
